Week 2: Music as Notation

Linger by The Cranberries transcribed ->

In this week's task, I transcribed Linger by The Cranberries. Orignially this arrangement was for synthesizer and piano, but I transposed it to voice and piano instead. Whilst transforming the score there were multiple problems I faced:

  • Added an extra bar of measures at the beginning of the original score, I was unable to remove these without deleting the seconnd bar of measures as well.
  • Changed fret board diagrams which turned the single notes with slurs into a barré, I changes this back but was unable to add the slur notation on top.
  • Minor notations were added such as a double cleff and count numbers above measures - these were deleted
  • The final measure's lyrics were altered and needed to be fixed.
